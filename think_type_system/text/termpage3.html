EMOTIV is the pioneer of simple to use, high quality, mobile EEG technologies.
EMOTIV is a bioinformatics company advancing understanding of the human brain using electroencephalography (EEG). The mission is to empower individuals to understand their own brain and accelerate brain research globally.

*Reference: https://www.emotiv.com/about-emotiv/


Emotiv Neuroheadsets

Emotiv EPOC+: A hi-resolution 14 channel mobile EEG used for contextualized scientific research grade results.

Emotiv Insight: A prosumer 5 channel mobile EEG used by engaged individuals seeking better understanding of their brains and mental states.
